### The OSS survey

The OSS-BHKi survey report details findings from our community about their awareness and engagement with Open Source Software. We present these findings in hopes of collaborating with other open science and source communities, especially in the life sciences, to strengthen our community members' skills and contributions.

Read the full report here: <a href="https://github.com/karegapauline/BHKi-projects/blob/main/OSS-BHKi%20survey%20report.docx">OSS-BHKi survey report</a>

So we ask for collaboration to fill in the gaps where we cannot do it alone in the following ways: 

1. Give a talk about OSS: this can be introductory material about OSS, about tool packaging, and more. 
2. Share an event: hybrid or in person.
3. Come do a training: beginner to advanced
4. Be a mentor: this entails an open invitation to fireside chats, which can be group or 1v1 sessions guided by questions

So, if this sounds like you, reach out to us via email.
